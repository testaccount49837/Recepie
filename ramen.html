Declicious Ramen at your Home

Easy homemade chicken ramen, with a flavorful broth, roasted chicken, fresh veggies, lots of noodles, and a soft cooked egg. Inspired by traditional Japanese ramen, but on the table in under an hour.

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Chicken
  • Veggies like Carrots, peas or kale
  • Eggs

Recepie Instructions

  1. Boil the chicken in a pot with water and salt.
  2. Boil the eggs in a separate pot.
  3. Boil the ramen noodles in a separate pot.
  4. Make the broth by boiling the chicken, garlic, ginger and veggies in a separate pot.
  5. Once the broth is ready, add the noodles and the eggs.
  6. Enjoy the delicious ramen.
